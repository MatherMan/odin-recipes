Beef Pasta

This is the world famous beef pasta by Riley. This dish is very simple and easy to make. The best part about it is it's vibrant flavor. Follow along to see how we make it.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Any kind
  • Rao's Marinara pasta sauce very important!
  • 1 lbs of ground beef
  • Frozen cheesey bread sticks

Instructions

  1. Follow instructions for frozen cheesey bread sticks
  2. Bring water to a boil
  3. Cook pasta in water
  4. Start a pan on medium heat
  5. Dice beef in pan to desire size
  6. Season beef and cook until brown
  7. Cook pasta till al dente
  8. Combine drained pasta, cooked beef, and sauce into pot and stir
  9. Serve, and enjoy
